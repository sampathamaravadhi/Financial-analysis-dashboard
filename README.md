# Financial Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)
This project is an interactive Financial Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI.
It analyzes Sales, Profit, Cost of Goods Sold (COGS), and Units Sold across countries,
segments, products, and discount bands to support business decision-making.

## Business Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to:
- Track overall financial performance of the company
- Identify high and low profit-generating countries and segments
- Analyze the impact of discount bands on profit, sales, and units sold
- Support management in making data-driven strategic decisions

## Key KPIs

- Total Sales: 118.73M
- Total Profit: 16.89M
- Total COGS: 101.83M
- Total Units Sold: 1.13M

## Dashboard Insights

### 1. Profit Analysis
- Government and Small Business segments contribute the highest profit
- Germany and France are top-performing countries by profit

### 2. Sales & COGS Analysis
- Sales and COGS are closely aligned, indicating controlled operational costs
- Certain products generate high revenue but lower profit margins

### 3. Discount Band Analysis
- Low and Medium discount bands generate higher profit compared to high discounts
- Excessive discounts negatively impact profitability despite higher units sold

### 4. Product Performance
- Paseo and VTT are top-performing products
- Some products have high sales volume but low profit contribution

  - COGS
  - Units Sold


## How to Use

1. Download the Power BI (.pbix) file from this repository
2. Open it using Power BI Desktop
3. Use slicers to filter by Year, Country, Segment, or Product
4. Analyze KPIs and visuals to extract business insights
